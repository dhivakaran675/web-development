About Us


CHOLAN MESS WAS OPENED AND OWNED BY P. DHIVAKARAN. IN THE START, IT WAS JUST A CART IN MADURAI, BUT NOW IT IS A RESTAURANT, AND IT ONLY SERVES CHETTINAD DISHES. THE AMBIENCE IS IN A RETRO SETUP. THIS IS THE INFORMATION ABOUT US.

OUR SPECIAL ITEMS ARE PORICHA PORATTA, MUTTON KOTTU PORATTA, SEERAGA SAMBA MUTTON BIRYANI, TENDER COCONUT PUDDING, PAYASAM, NOOL PORATTA, PICHI POTA NATU KOLI, CHETTINAD MUTTON GRAVY, GRAPE PULP JUICE, KIZHI PORATTA. THESE ARE OUR SPECIAL ITEMS.
